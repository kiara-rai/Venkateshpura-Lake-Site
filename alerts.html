Algal Bloom Advisory

High
Oct 12, 2025 Auto-sensor + Visual confirmation

Dense algal bloom observed near the northern inlet. Potential toxin production may cause skin irritation and harm to pets and wildlife.

Recommended actions:
  • Avoid contact with water; keep children and pets away.
  • Do not fish or use the water for irrigation until cleared.
  • Report wildlife distress through the reporting form below.

Dissolved Oxygen Dip Detected

Moderate
Oct 11, 2025 Water quality sensors

Dissolved oxygen levels are below optimal thresholds during early morning hours, increasing stress risks for fish.

Recommended actions:
  • Avoid any fish release activities this week.
  • Report fish gasping or surface clustering.
  • Volunteers: monitor morning conditions.

Shoreline Litter Accumulation (East Bank)

Low
Oct 10, 2025 Community report

Increased plastic and food waste found near the east walking trail entrance after weekend activity.

Recommended actions:
  • Use designated bins and carry trash out if bins are full.
  • Volunteers: join the Saturday clean-up (9:00 AM).
  • Report overflowing bins via the form below.
