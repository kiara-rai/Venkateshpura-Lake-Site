Report an Issue

What to report

  • Pollution: oil, chemicals, foam, unusual odors, or discoloration in water.
  • Littering: plastic, glass, construction debris, or dumping activities.
  • Wildlife disturbance: injured animals, illegal fishing, trapping, or feeding.
  • Water quality: sudden fish deaths, algal blooms, or unusual turbidity.
  • Other concerns affecting safety, access, or habitat around the lake.
